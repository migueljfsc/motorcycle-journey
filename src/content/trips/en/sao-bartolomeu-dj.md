A hectic one. Sofia and I left at 7 AM and drove down to Coimbra to paddle 11 km down the Mondego, from a put-in upstream to the river beach at Palheiros e Zorro. We had lunch there, then drove back to Porto. I grabbed my bike and headed out to São Bartolomeu do Mar in Esposende, met up with some friends, and we ran our own DJ set on the beach until sunset.
