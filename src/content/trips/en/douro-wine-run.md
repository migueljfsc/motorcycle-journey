The N222 along the Douro is regularly voted one of the best driving roads in the world, and
on two wheels it's even better. Terraced vineyards dropping to the river, corner after
corner, and a glass of port waiting at the end (saved for after the ride, of course).

## The road

Tight, technical, and shaded in places — the CL500's light steering earns its keep here.
Stopped at half a dozen miradouros just to take it all in.
