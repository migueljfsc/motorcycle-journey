A spring blast along the south coast before the summer crowds arrive. Cliff-top roads in
the west, gentle farmland in the east, and a tailwind most of the way.

## The day

Started at the dramatic cliffs near Lagos, worked east through Faro, and finished in Tavira
with the bike covered in salt and a grin to match.
