A loop through Peneda-Gerês, the only national park in Portugal. Waterfalls, granite peaks,
wild horses on the road, and some of the smoothest mountain tarmac in the north.

## Highlights

The climb past the Vilarinho das Furnas reservoir, and a long lunch stop in a village
that felt like it hadn't changed in a century.
