A single big day in the highest mountains in mainland Portugal. Cold at the top even in
May, glorious in the valleys. The kind of ride that justifies the whole pre-season service.

## The climb

Switchback after switchback out of Covilhã. The CL500 is in its element here — light
enough to flick through the tight stuff, torquey enough not to drop a gear every corner.

## The top

Snow patches still clinging to the north faces. Stopped for coffee that went cold in
about ninety seconds. Worth it for the view back down the glacial valley.
