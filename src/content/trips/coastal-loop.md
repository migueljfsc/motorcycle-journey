The plan was simple: stay west, chase the coast road, and don't take a single motorway
unless absolutely forced. Three days, one tent, and a tankbag full of optimism.

## Day 1 — Porto to Figueira da Foz

Left at dawn to beat the city traffic. The N109 hugs the coast in fits and starts —
some stretches are postcard-perfect with the Atlantic on your right, others dump you
into roundabout-laden suburbs. Worth it for the seafood lunch in Aveiro.

## Day 2 — The good roads

This was the day. Inland detour through the Serra, then back to the coast. Empty tarmac,
long sweepers, and a headwind strong enough to lean into on the straights.

## Day 3 — Land's end

Sagres at last. Stood at the cliffs where the old maps said the world ended. Turned the
bike around and started planning the next one before I'd even left the car park.
