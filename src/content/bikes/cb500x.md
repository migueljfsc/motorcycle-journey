The do-everything middleweight adventure-tourer. Bought it as a first "big" bike for the
A2 licence and never grew out of it — commute on Monday, gravel forest road on Saturday.

Mods so far: crash bars, a taller touring screen, and a centre stand that makes chain
maintenance painless. The 471cc twin won't win drag races but it'll happily sit at
motorway speed all day and still return 28 km/L.
