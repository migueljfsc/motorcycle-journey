The first tour I over-packed badly. Half of it came back unused and the extra weight made
the bike a chore in the twisties. Here's the pared-down list that survived.

## Always packs

- Tyre repair kit + mini compressor — the single most useful thing you can carry.
- One spare base layer; rinse and dry the rest overnight.
- Compact rain gear, even if the forecast is clear.

## Stays home

- "Just in case" tools you don't know how to use.
- A second pair of boots. One pair, worn.
- Anything you could buy cheaply on the road if you truly needed it.

The rule: every item must earn its volume. If you hesitate, it stays.
